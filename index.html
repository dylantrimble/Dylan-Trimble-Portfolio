About

I am a fullstack web developer, with a certificate of completion from the full stack web development bootcamp at Univversity of North Carolina Charlotte. My interest in code began long before completing the Bootcamp. In the summer of 2018 I began to teach myself how to write code through various resources such as CodeCademy, Treehouse, FreeCodeCamp, YouTube, and so many other resources. In this time, I learned how to overcome frustration and how to google the questions I couldn't find the answer to. My interest and love for code grew the more I learned. That's when I realized that I would need more than just the knowhow to land my dream job. So I looked into furthering my education and enrolled in the bootcamps at UNNC


As much as I love technology and web development, those aren't my only interests. I have a great passion for cars, coffee, and music. I've spent countless hours both professionally and as a hobby in all of those realms, however none have them have kept me interested the way code has in a professional capacity. In my free time I still tinker on my car and surround my self in car culture. With continuos growth of knowledge, my network, and my resources, I have also begun to submurge myself in the coding world by going to meetups, working on projects both alone and as a group in my spare time, while also learning new languages and technologies to further my career development.
